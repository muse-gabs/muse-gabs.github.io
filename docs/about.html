About Me

I graduated from the University of Washington with a Bachelor of Arts in Korean and a Bachelor of Science in Applied Physics in 2018. I also graduated from Oregon State University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science in 2021. Prior to graduation, I was working as a Teaching Assistant for CS 340, the Introduction to Databases Course. In that course, the students are tasked with developing a project website from start to finish. This involves developing a project plan, creating a schema, providing CRUD functionalities, and ending with a completed website. I assisted with grading the project steps and providing feedback to help their projects progress smoothly.

I occasionally watch anime and enjoy listening to kpop. I also love learning different languages. In my spare time, I sometimes play rhythm games, watch twitch streams, or draw. Depending on the season, I like going skiing or scuba diving.
